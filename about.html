Hi I'm Sara!

My love for design stems from various forms of inspiration, which I pull from my personal experiences growing up in Wisconsin and living in other states. My favorite part about design is that it's constantly evolving and I love helping to tell a story. Everyone has a unique story to tell and I put my talent into telling that story through meaningful design.

I am an ambitious, creative and talented freelance graphic designer currently based out of southeastern WI. I am a self-starter with strong organizational, analytical, communication and customer relation skills in addition to problem solving. I love working independently and collaboratively as part of a team.
